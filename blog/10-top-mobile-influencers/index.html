10 Mobile Influencers You Should Follow

17 June 2015 on Product

Staying on top of the latest developments in the mobile market is core to be successful. Learning about the latest trends, participating in discussions about the future and knowing the hottest new technologies is necessary to be a relevant player in this ever-changing environment.

We have collected 10 mobile influencers that inspire us and shape the discussions in the mobile industry. Follow them to always be on top of the ecosystem.

1. Benedict Evans, @BenedictEvans

It seems Benedict Evans is one of the smartest guys on earth, having an incredible overview on the whole mobile ecosystem and where it's going. He is blogging and publishes a weekly newsletter which we highly recommend.

2. Luke Wroblewski, @lukew

Luke Wroblewski is Product Director at Google and one of the leading product minds on the internet. Being author of Mobile First his thoughts were and are influencing what mobile products look like today.

3. Dan Kaplan, @dankaplan

Dan Kaplan is all about growth. He helped amazing companies like Twilio, Asana and Salesforce to get their marketing right. He is also columnist at TechCrunch and writes a blog.

4. Maribel Lopez, @maribellopez

Maribel Lopez founded Lopez Research, a leading mobile market research firm. She writes about how mobile changes the world on the Mobility Blog and is author at Forbes.

5. Ina Fried, @inafried

Journalist Ina Friend writes excellent pieces on everything mobile at Re/code. Make sure to follow her and you won't miss out on the latest trends of the industry.

6. Andrew Chen, @andrewchen

Andrew Chen has written more than 650 essays on mobile, user growth and fundraising. We recommend to subscribe to his newsletter to receive his latest pieces straight to your inbox.

7. Peggy Anne Salz, @peggyanne

Peggy Anne Salz is analyst and author, leading the technology site Mobile Groove. She is also author at Forbes and writes about mobile marketing, engagement and apps.

8. Peter Kafka, @pkafka

Peter Kafka is a widely regarded tech journalist who is writing for Re/code. Having worked for several tech media houses his articles are always thoughtful reads that we recommend.

9. Kevin C. Tofel, @kevinctofel

Kevin C. Tofel writes about the mobile industry since 2003. He covers mobile platforms, apps and services on ZDNet and we highly recommend his articles, analyzes and podcasts.

10. Benjamin Robbins, @paladorbenjamin

Last but not least, we recommend to follow Benjamin Robbins. He spent an entire year solely working from a single mobile device and regularly writes on everything mobile for The Guradian.

Do you have other awesome influencers we should not miss? Head over to Twitter and let us know!
